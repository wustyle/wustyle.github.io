Orbital Component


Orbital Component spawns and manages auto resizeable arrays of actors. It's useful for abilities with telekinetic type powers and planetary motion systems.


Inspiration


Inspired by a story from wuxia world, the protagonist Quing Shui's family martial art spawned blue lotus' that they could telekinetically move with their mind. They moved these around to block or attack in physical space. I wanted to design it so the player could co-ordinate attack patterns like macros and have them play out with a single button click. Later on as Qing Shui's blood evolved through the ancient strengthening technique, his lotus became golden.
